about me

Hi, I'm Sof. My passion for photography started with my love for travel and connecting with people. There is something special in being able to capture the beauty in a moment, no matter how random or significant it is.

My camera is my window to the world, allowing me to be more aware of my surroundings and acknowledge the beauty in the most ordinary things! Today, I use my photography skills to help people immortalize the special moments in their lives and brands to show their full potential online.
