My photography journey started with my passion for travel. When I was a kid we moved to Africa due to my dad's work and, since then, the desire to discover new places and cultures has stayed with me

Somewhere along my travels and life abroad, I decided to pick up a camera, but it was only during my 2-year backpacking trip around this curiously unique country, Australia, that my camera became my most powerful storytelling tool. It pushes me to be more aware of my surroundings and helps me connect with people

Then covid hit and, like everyone else, I had to adapt. Traveling was not an option anymore so I started expanding my photography portfolio to venue and food photography, promoting the cafe I was working for at the time

Fast forward a few years, many traveled kilometers, and a series of nerve-racking lockdowns and you will now see me using my photography to share travel/street stories, help people immortalize special moments in their lives, and brands showcase their full potential online
