Somewhere along my travels and life abroad, I decided to pick up a camera, but it was only during my 2-year backpacking trip around Australia and its unique landscape, that my camera became my most powerful storytelling tool. When covid hit, like everyone else, I had to adapt. Traveling was not an option anymore so I started expanding my photography portfolio to food & venue photography, promoting the cafe I was working for at the time. I also took this time to learn how to code with the amazing Institute of Code to be able to build this website from scratch

As I've always loved working with people and wanted to expand my skills into portrait photography, this led me to a position of Newborn & Family Photographer with Mishka Studios

Since 2022, I've had the priviledge of working with over 600 babies and their families, at Frances Perry House and other major Melbourne maternity hospitals. Not only do I get to capture their first days of life, but also other future milestones
