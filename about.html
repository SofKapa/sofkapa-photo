Beautiful photos are important, but it's the connection with the person behind the lense that will make your experience even more unforgettable

One of the great advantages of living in Melbourne is the abundance of creative talent available. When it comes to choosing your photographer, the challenge is to find someone who truly understands your vibe, and delivers photos that feel authentic and true to you or your brand

about me

Hi, I’m Sof! A Czech-Portuguese photographer living in the land Down Under

My photography journey started with my passion for travel . When I was a kid my family and I moved to Cape Verde for my dad's work and, since then, the desire to discover new places and cultures has never left me. Somewhere along my travels and life abroad, I decided to pick up a camera, but it was only during my 2-year backpacking trip around Australia and its unique landscape, that my camera became my most powerful storytelling tool. It pushes me to be more aware of my surroundings and helps me connect with people

When covid hit, like everyone else, I had to adapt. Traveling was not an option anymore so I started expanding my photography portfolio to food & venue photography, promoting the cafe I was working for at the time. I also took this time to learn how to code with the amazing Institute of Code to be able to build this website from scratch

As I've always loved working with people and wanted to expand my skills into portrait photography, this led me to a position of Newborn & Family Photographer with Mishka Studios. Since 2022, I've had the priviledge of working with over 600 babies and their families, at Frances Perry House and other major Melbourne maternity hospitals.
