Beautiful photos are important, but it's the connection with the person behind the lense that will make your experience even more unforgettable

Someone who sees the way your kid tugs your sleeve when they’re tired. Who notices the way the light hits your café counter just before the morning rush. Who’s tuned into the moments that would usually slip by

about me

Hey, I’m Sof. Czech-Portuguese, based in Melbourne, and interested in the moments that don’t ask for attention — but deserve it anyway

Photography didn’t start as a career. It started as curiosity. I grew up moving countries, and somewhere between Cape Verde and what was meant to be a one-year backpacking trip around Australia, I picked up a camera and never really put it down

It’s how I tune in — with people, places and, most of all, moments

When COVID grounded everything, I swapped plane tickets for café shifts and, as things got back to "normal", I started capturing the energy of venues, food, and the people behind them

I also built this website from scratch (thanks to a deep dive into coding with the legends at the Institute of Code), and focused on what’s always felt right — honest, human storytelling

Gradually, that led me into portrait photography — and for the past few years, I’ve had the absolute honour of working with over 600 babies and their families across Melbourne’s maternity wards, in-home and on-location

From tiny hands and toes to big, messy milestones — it all matters
