Cookie Policy

Last updated: 25 April 2025

I value your privacy very highly. Please read this Cookie Policy carefully before using the Sof Kapa Photography Website (the "Website") operated by Sof Kapa ("I" "me" "my"), as this Cookie Policy contains important information regarding our use of cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your browsing experience, understand how our website is used, and help us create better content for you. This policy also provides information regarding your privacy and how we may use the information that we collect about you

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember things like your preferences or what pages you’ve visited.

2. How I use cookies?

I use cookies and similar tools for the following purposes:

Analytics & Performance

I use Google Analytics (GA4) and Google Tag Manager (GTag) to understand how visitors use my website — which pages are most popular, how people find me, and what I can do to improve

Website Functionality

Tools like Calendly and HubSpot Live Chat use cookies so that features like booking a session or chatting with me run smoothly

Marketing & Tracking

I may use tracking pixels (like Facebook Pixel and Pinterest Tag, and possibly others in the future) to help measure the effectiveness of my marketing and offer relevant content or ads on other platforms

Search Insights

I use Google Search Console to monitor how my site appears in search results and to improve SEO performance

3. Your choices

When you first visit my website, you’ll see a cookie banner where you can choose to accept our Cookie Policy. You can also adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies at any time

4. Updates

I may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as we add or change tools on my website. The latest version will always be available here.

5. Questions

If you have any questions about this Cookie Policy and how I use cookies, feel free to reach out through my contact page or chat with me directly on the site via the live chat
